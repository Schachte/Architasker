Architasker Overview

The personal assistant at the tip of your fingers

Automatic Time Based Scheduling

The main part of Architasker is to analyze the events you currently have scheduled and compare them against events you need to complete. the system will asks some basic questions such as percent to complete by end of week and priority to automatically fix events into your calendar easily and efficiently.

Integration With Google Calendar

Architasker has allowed integration with Google to pull in existing and recurring events that may be used by their system. Our system will analyze such events and incorporate them into the scheduling system.

Conflict Warning & Analysis

When times conflict or there is not enough time allocated to complete your task list, architaskr will warn you to re-evaluate your tasks. This is calculated using an efficiency algorithm based on priority and other key factors.

Continuous Notifcations

Optionally, you will be able to receive live notifications for tasks via SMS or Email. This provides a more "assistant-like" feel to the system, allowing you to be on task and aware of your life schedule at all times.

Student Friendly

Architasker was designed by students to efficiently organize and plan schedules. The system has a myriad of features useful to students such as break-time reccommendations, pomodoro timer integration and more!

Simple UI

Here at Architasker, we wanted to keep things simple. Although the work behind the scenes is complex, we kept the user experience, design and interaction with our application simple and easy to use.
