Overview

Architasker is a productivity management application for the student, lifehacker, professional or average-joe. Simply add your weekly events (or sync them from Google Calendar), add your tasks you need to complete and we do the rest. Architasker has an advanced randomized distribution algorithm that will analyze your calendar and insert events based on priority, percent to complete, due date and more!

Events

Events represent things such as attending a party, class, work or daily routines that you are sure are going to happen in your week at pre-defined times.

Tasks

Tasks are the bulk of the application. Tasks are things that you need to complete over some period of time. These tasks can include things such as:

  • Running
  • Complete Math Homework
  • Clear My Emails
  • Study For Math Test
  • Plan My Meeting
